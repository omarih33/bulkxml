Import to Squarespace

In Squarespace, go to Settings > Advanced > Import/Export > Import. Choose WordPress as the source platform, then toggle to the Advanced tab. Drop the XML file you’ve gotten from WordPress there and click Begin Import. After a few minutes, you’ll see a Success message. Imported content will show up under the Not Linked section.
