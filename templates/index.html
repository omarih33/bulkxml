Import to Squarespace

In Squarespace, go to Settings > Advanced > Import/Export > Import.
Choose WordPress as the source platform, then toggle to the Advanced tab.
Drop the XML file you just downloaded and click Begin Import.
After a few minutes, you’ll see a Success message.
Imported content will show up under the Not Linked section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What does the CSV to XML converter do?
Our tool converts a CSV file containing your blog posts into an XML format that can be easily imported into Squarespace, allowing you to upload multiple blog posts at once.

2. What should I do if my XML file fails to import?
First, ensure that your CSV file is correctly formatted according to our template. If you continue to experience issues, double-check that you’ve selected WordPress as the source platform in Squarespace and that you’ve toggled to the Advanced tab before uploading the XML file.

3. Can I import posts into an existing blog?
Yes, you can import posts into an existing blog roll in Squarespace. The imported posts will appear in the Not Linked section, where you can move them to your desired blog roll.

4. What happens to the images in my blog posts?
Images linked in your CSV file will be included in the import, but make sure the URLs are correct and accessible. If an image fails to upload, you might need to manually update it within Squarespace after the import.

5. How long does the import process take?
The import process typically takes a few minutes, but this can vary depending on the size of your blog and the speed of your internet connection. You’ll receive a Success message once the import is complete.

6. I need help! How can I get support?
If you encounter any issues or have questions, feel free to reach out to our support team. We’re here to help you every step of the way.
